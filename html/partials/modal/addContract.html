SELL: Certified Merchant Program

To join the Certified Merchant program:

  • You must be a licensed and established business.
    Please note: currently Bidz.com does not support private sellers

  • You must agree to the following Terms & Conditions:
       - You must sell your merchandise starting at $1.00 No Minimum Reserve Price.
       - You must provide prompt & professional customer service.
       - You must agree to a 15 day money-back guarantee!
       - You must ship out item(s) 3-5 days after payment(s) is received.
       - You must have product description and images available.

  • You must accept OpenBazaar selling fees.To qualify, you must fill out and submit the form below.
NOTICE:

1. You must have your product in stock and ready to be shipped.

2. We do not accept applications for the following categories:

   - Jewelry       - 3 Minute Auctions       - Vacations/Travel

3. We will no longer accept applications for merchants selling items through "dropship companies" or "distributing companies" (Companies that sell items from catalogs, and/or other companies dropship the products)
